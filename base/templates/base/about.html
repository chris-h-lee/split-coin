What is Split Coin?

Split Coin is a way for volunteers and donators to connect with charity organizations on a smaller scale. We created this web application with the hope that removing the pressure of large time and financial commitments would encourage more people to support good causes. As such we created a platform where people can easily find causes they support and enable them to contribute however they can.

What are the goals of Split Coin?

Currently, Split Coin is a donation and volunteering web application based in Charlottesville, Virginia which allows local residents to connect with local charities. However, in the future we hope to expand this service to allow people across the world to support global causes. We also intend to add more quality of life features such as a filtering feature, allowing users to customize the opportunites they see, and a credibiliy score for organizations.
